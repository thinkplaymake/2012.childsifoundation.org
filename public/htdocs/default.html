Polly is two.

She's been
abandoned but
Polly is not
an orphan

Every day, children like Polly are abandoned in Uganda, left to fend for themselves in ditches and empty houses.
In fact, 80% of abandoned children still have a family. We believe all children, like Polly, should grow up in a loving family.

Childs i Foundation
reunites abandoned children with their families,
instead of sending them to orphanages...

After spending a few months in our transitional home regaining her strength, we located Polly's family. They welcomed her into their home with open arms.

If we hadn't found Polly's family, we would have found her a new family in Uganda.

We find foster and adoptive homes in Uganda for other children like Polly, where they are raised smiling, thriving and healthy.
