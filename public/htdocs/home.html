Polly is two

She's been abandoned, but Polly is not an orphan

Every week, children like Polly are abandoned in Uganda, left to fend for themselves in ditches and empty houses.
In fact, 80% of abandoned children in orphanages have family. We believe all children, like Polly, should grow up in a loving family

Childs i Foundation
reunites abandoned children with their families,
instead of sending them to orphanages...

Polly spent a few months in our transitional home regaining her strength.

We then traced and found Polly's family, who welcomed her with open arms.

If we couldn't have found her family, we would have found a new foster or adoptive home within Uganda.

Our approach works
long term

Instead of being raised in orphanages, we aim to find families for all children - over 80 and counting
